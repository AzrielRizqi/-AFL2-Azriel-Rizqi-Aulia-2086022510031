Avenged Sevenfold

One of the more successful and accessible metalcore band of the early 21st century, Avenged Sevenfold's wide-ranging sound encompasses mosh-pit-ready hardcore, progressive-leaning alt-metal, and anthemic hard rock.
The band endured changes, both stylistic and internal, during their rise.
